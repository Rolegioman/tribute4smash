What is Smash?

Super Smash Brothers is a crossover fighting game series published by Nintendo 22 years ago that primarily features characters from various Nintendo franchises, it was created by Masahiro Sakurai who has directed every game in the series being Ultimate the last of them, the game was meant to be a crossover for only Nintendo IP's but everything changed in SSB Brawl when Sonic (character from Sega, Nintendo's direct competitor) and Snake (fom Metal Gear Solid) were added as guest stars, from then the game would generate tons of hype in all video game community to see which character could be invited to the game.

Gameplay

The game differs from most fighting games, the main objective is to knock enemies out of the stage instead of lowering their HP.

Moves

As I said before the game is very different from others, and that applies to the way you make your character move while in other titles you have to make 4 directional inputs and press 2 or 3 buttons at the same time for your character to throw a punch to the ribs and finish with an uppercut, here you just have to make one directional input; <---side--->, up, down and pres the attack button or special, or you could just use the attck or special button on its own attack button and your character will do the rest.

Common Questions

  • Does it has competitive?

    Sadly it does have

  • How is a party game turned into a competitive game?

    Easy, remove all the fun and make stupid rules, like "No items allowed, but you can use Hero, Diddy Kong, Peach/Daisy or R.O.B because they absolutely don't use items"

  • Is this a good Game?

    It is an excellent game, but of course it's got flaws, like the competitive and "casual" as they call it matchmakers are united, so you'll end up against a "pro" or a "casual" even if you don't want to

  • How are the servers?

    You know, I really love this game, but I gotta be honest, they suck, even if you are connected to a LAN, you'll get lag or get disconnected and punished for that and... you have to pay for it.

  • Is online the only way to have fun?

    If you're one of those self-proclaimed "pros" then yes, if not, there is the world of light (story mode), classic game, mob smash, spirit board, training, you can have a lot of fun with those.

  • How do I choose a character?

    Pick the one who catches your eye, doesn't matter if you haven't played the original game, being honest, I doubt everyone has played all the games represented by the fighters .
